About

Doing the right thing at the right time.

CyberTeam provides training in Cyber Security and Digital Forensics for various public & private organisations. Our team is lead by the folks who work with Indian Air Force and Indian Police. Our goal is to spread awareness about Cyber Security and make as many as people equipped with the tools and knowledge to defend themselves from different kind of cyber threats. CyberTeam connects various institutes and Cyber Security trainers to provide training in Cyber Security, Penetration Testing, Digital Forensics and Fraud Investigations. Our training includes live projects and hands-on experience with various security tools. Our team is dedicated to spreading cyber awareness among people so that they can know the value of their privacy.
